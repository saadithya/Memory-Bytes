Permissions Required

  • Camera Access: To capture photos of items for the inventory
  • Photos/Media Storage: To access and save item photos to your device
  • External Storage Read/Write: To store item images and manage the inventory database
  • Audio Recording: For potential voice note functionality (future enhancement)
  • Internet Access: To connect with AI services (OpenAI/Gemini) for item recognition

How It Works

  1. Add Items: Take photos of your belongings and add them to your digital inventory
  2. Categorize: Assign appropriate categories and add location details
  3. Track: Use search and filter functions to locate items quickly
  4. AI Enhancement: Use AI features to automatically detect and catalog items from photos
  5. Export Data: Securely backup your inventory for safekeeping

Privacy & Security

Your data remains on your device and is never shared with third parties. All inventory information is stored locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security of your personal belongings information.
